The government of Tanini has taken special measures to enhance the fulfillment of all recommended health and safety measures due to COVID-19. Follow all posted instructions while visiting our beautiful local attractions and small businesses while at Tanini. As recommended by WHO and the US CDC, face masks and coverings are required. As well as social distancing of at least 6 feet between persons. Please make sure to wash hands often or use approved 70% alcohol based hand sanitizers in the event running water and soap is unavailable. An inherent risk of exposure to COVID-19 exists in any public space where people are present. Stay safe!(Disney, 2020)
